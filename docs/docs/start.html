Basic functionality and core entities

The most basic use of Newspack Ads is all about connecting a GAM account and configuring global ad slots using ad units fetched from GAM:

At an entity level, GAM is a Provider , and the ad slots are possible through Placements . These 2 entities are the foundation of the plugin and their extensibility makes our features and 3rd party integrations possible.
